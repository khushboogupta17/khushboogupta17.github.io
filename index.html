About me

Delhi based Unity Game Developer and Designer who spends her days and nights with a laptop.

My knack of building creative stuff brought me into game development and this world has lot to explore.I have experience in both 2D and 3D games .My development approach in the work I create is tilted towards creativity and simplicity.My curiosity to learn new things and develope unique stuff keeps me going.

The best part about my journey? I learnt everything by myself.I watched online videos and then started my experiments on the software.Having no mentor to look up to during errors was definiely a challenging phase,I have even spent two months figuring out very common error.But this all made me who I am,now I help and teach other developers to solve their bugs in game.

I have tried my hands at Augmented Reality to understand how it works and made both PC and mobile games in unity.For building games my weapons of choice are Unity,Photoshop and Visual Studio.I have done several projects with some of them uploaded on playstore.If I'm (!in front of laptop) then I must be drawing a scene from the world I dreamt of.
